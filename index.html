FOOD

Definition

Food is any substance consumed to supply nutritional support for an organism. Food is typically of plant, animal or fungal origin, and contains essential nutrients, like carbohydrates, fats, proteins, vitamins, or minerals. The substance is ingested by an organism and assimilated by the organism's cells to produce energy, maintain life, or stimulate growth.
